Title: Short Travel Guide to Tokyo

Tokyo is a large, modern city with efficient public transportation.
The JR Yamanote Line circles many major districts like Shinjuku, Shibuya, and Ueno.

Highlights:
- Shinjuku: skyscrapers, nightlife, and Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden.
- Asakusa: Senso-ji Temple and traditional shopping streets.
- Akihabara: electronics shops and anime culture.
- Odaiba: a futuristic bay area with malls and waterfront views.

Practical tips:
- Get an IC card like Suica or Pasmo for trains and buses.
- Most small restaurants expect cash, though cards are becoming more common.
- Many places provide free Wi-Fi, but a pocket Wi-Fi router is convenient.
